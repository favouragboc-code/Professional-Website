About

A results-driven Agricultural Economist with over seven years of progressive experience advancing agricultural innovation, environmental resilience, and governance systems across Nigeria.

Currently serving with the Nigeria Plant Variety Protection Office (NPVPO), I implement national and international frameworks that safeguard plant breeders' rights, promote biodiversity, and strengthen agricultural adaptation to climate risks.

I have collaborated with global partners including CIMMYT, AGRA, the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, and the African Seed Industry, contributing to the IMAGE project and the Nigeria-Netherlands Collaborative Seed Program.

Motivated by a deep commitment to sustainability and equity, I am currently pursuing an MSc in Climate Change and Economic Policy at Ahmadu Bello University — building capacity to design and implement transformative solutions at the intersection of climate science and food security.
